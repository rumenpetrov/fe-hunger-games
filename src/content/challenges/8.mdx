<Section>
  ## Requirements
  * Develop [custom element](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/Web_components/Using_custom_elements). Make it as simple or as complex as you like.
  * Isolate your element by using a [shadow DOM](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/Web_components/Using_custom_elements#examples). No styles should be leaking out of your web component.
  * Write it in plain JS. (Don't use 3th-party libraries like [Lit](https://lit.dev/))
  * <Special /> Use at least 1 [CSS custom variable](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/CSS/Using_CSS_custom_properties) to style something inside your web component from outside of the shadow DOM
</Section>
